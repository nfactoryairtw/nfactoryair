About Us

Establish in 2013, from a dinner table where everyone dine together, members of Neng Sheng Xing Factory gradually form themselves a commune and a quasi family. 26 year-old on average, these subjects focus on nature environments, promoting their ideas by organizing various social movements in form of parade, exhibitions, forums, etc. These people are making progress urging articles that considered more just to be passed regarding eco-friendly, no fertilizer farming and LGBT rights.
For a project of discovering and reconstructing the subjectivity of Neng Sheng Xing Factory, N-Factory Artist in Residency program Set up in 2016. With an imagination of the curatorial, we organized three exhibitions under related topics: from global to local, and finally the affect which the group initiated. The factory offers residencies, studios, technical supports, local network and profound ties within connections with people. Having expectation that art could be a method for people to address certain issues in a more subjective way, we invite artists, curators, and other people who work in the field of arts and culture. As all participants interact and to live together in the factory, their production, through language of art, will expand new dimensions of perceiving things. By organizing various cultural events in a global perspective, we encourage more art projects that contain vitality and deep reflection in humanities.
We are looking forward to have a great time researching, creating, and exhibition making with all potential residents and collaborators.

《Perspectives of Individual》

Artists│Kaya Hanasaki, Yui Kao
Curator│Chiu Po-Ting, Showzoo Tsai
Term│08/28 Sun. 19:30～21:30

We are standing in the N-Factory.
One person is staring into my eyes.
One person is watching the point at where I am gazing.
One person is paying attention to our holding of hands.
One person is overlaying their own childhood memory onto the fabric, on which we are stepping.

Contrary to the intentions of the artist or curator, the audience is sharing artwork each within their own viewpoint and perspective. However, those viewpoints or perspectives are lurking in the shadows of the concept or critic, which is dictated by specialists.

Instead of letting it flow away, this performance invites the audience to participate to share their own viewpoint and perspective by filming and writing about the performance. We will share those videos and writings during a coming exhibition at N-Factory in October. 

Please bring your devices for shooting. We are also live broadcasting our performance on line. Anyone can participate on this work through the Internet.

柏林行為藝術工作坊

活動日期│2016年6月18日～6月19日
能盛興工廠藝術村
邀請長駐柏林與德國藝術家進行國際交流

行為藝術是一個使用身體，橫跨時間與空間，理性和感性的藝術表達方式。它跳脫媒介，任何藝術形式皆可存在其中，一種無限大的的可能性。不同于其它藝術形式的物質創造，它同時運用邏輯性概念思考和感官靈性，觸發觀者的的認知并開啟共同創造心得。是種對於個人心靈和社會現況的反射和療愈，一種人類共通語言的溝通程序和經驗分享。

在此工作坊中，由柏林行為藝術協會會長 Lan 和德國行為藝術家 Joy 帶領，從如何從理性和邏輯思考分析身體的可能性，同時感性體驗心理和慾望在行為藝術上的應用，觀察和使用空間和時間元素，與其他藝術表現方式互相比較，從而建立個人行為藝術語言。

Partner

APAB（DE）

APAB understands performance art to be the sum of known and newly emerging practices of process based, transformative, integrative and intermedia art that can be presented live. The association places special importance on the development of new and experimental forms of performance art.
KLEX（MY）

KLEX is an independent, artist-run grassroots international festival of experimental film, video art and music founded in Malaysia in 2010 by a cross-disciplinary group of artists.KLEX aims to serve as a platform to introduce contemporary experimental cinema and music from the region and worldwide to the Malaysian audience, as well as to introduce works from South East Asia to other parts of the world.

Stattberlin（DE）

stattberlin is an art space dedicated to new forms of art expressions. We encourage site-specific projects, political commitment and emerging artists from all over the world, to make constructive and dynamic exchanges between artists and the public on the subject of the modern society and contemporary arts and the relations between traditional artistic standards.

Lostgens’（MY）

Lostgens established in early 2004. It provides an alternative space for budding innovative exhibitions and performances. Lostgens has entered a next phase of unique development and operation.Lostgens aims to weave itself into the multicultural artistic atmosphere. More than just a place that brings together artists, it also provides a platform for a dynamic growth of multi-layered artistic culture.
Baan Noorg（TH）

Baan Noorg Collaborative Arts and Culture is a non profit artist initiative, a fundament strategy for community development and a service-providing program. Baan Noorg functions as an alternative interdisciplinary art program for developing contemporary art and cultural production for local and global community.
