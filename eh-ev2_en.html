♦ About workshop

In the edge of our word there is a s filled up with waste materials , most people that survives , lives here on this flat land.

Because money will be useless in the future word, only gold and silver are the circulate currency ,they dig waist materials from the ground and exchange to daily necessities ; build houses with driftwood by the ocean , oil is used up , only bicycles to transfer to places. Theres a funnel standing up tall and strait , uses ocean current generation in the far , exhausting numerous fresh air . Future word can only rely on this huge funnel to circulate oxygen emission .

I digged a few bags of dirt here but no trace of fortune found ：utensils from primitive man, there were bowls ,cups and plates , human kind learned how to use fire to make pottery , the New Stone Age , cinder rock pieces are the result of their civilization , brilliant ceramic technology ,and they mark their own signature sign on the utensils. , what makes people astonish is the colors on theses prehistoric utensils that glaze like glass.

What made Prehistorical word the way it is !!!! lets produce the products of the rest of the human civilization back to the future.
