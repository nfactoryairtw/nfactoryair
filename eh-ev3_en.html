Perspectives of Indivisual

Artist│Kaya Hanasaki, Yui Kao 
Curator│Chiu Po-Ting, Showzoo Tsai
Term│08/28 Sun. 19:30～21:30


We are standing in the N-Factory.
One person is staring into my eyes.
One person is watching the point at where I am gazing.
One person is paying attention to our holding of hands.
One person is overlaying their own childhood memory onto the fabric, on which we are stepping.

Contrary to the intentions of the artist or curator, the audience is sharing artwork each within their own viewpoint and perspective. However, those viewpoints or perspectives are lurking in the shadows of the concept or critic, which is dictated by specialists.

Instead of letting it flow away, this performance invites the audience to participate to share their own viewpoint and perspective by filming and writing about the performance. We will share those videos and writings during a coming exhibition at N-Factory in October. 

Please bring your devices for shooting. We are also live broadcasting our performance on line. Anyone can participate on this work through the Internet.
